Dual degree in Mathematical engineering and computer engineering. Overview and subjects covered in the degree.

This is where all subjects taught through the first year of this degree are explained and ordered

Subjects covered: First Year, first four-month period:

  • Algebra I
  • Calculus I
  • Programming I
  • Logic and critical thinking
  • Fundamentals of computer engineering

Subjects covered during the second four-month period of the first year:

  • Algebra II
  • Discrete mathematics
  • Calculus II
  • History of science and technology
  • Computational and mathematical logic
  • Electronics and technology of computers

Subjects covered through all the first year

  • Knowledge management and person skills
